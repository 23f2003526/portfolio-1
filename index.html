About

I am Dr. Purnima Gupta, a dedicated educationist and researcher focused on language education, educational technology, and ICT integration in teaching and learning. With extensive academic background and leadership experience, I strive to advance pedagogical practices and educational policies that foster inclusive and innovative learning environments.
